Machine Learning Model Training

Steps to Follow:

  1. Click on the "Load Data" button to select and upload your dataset.
  2. To select the machine learning model, click on the respective button:
    • Logistic Regression
    • Support Vector Machine
    • Decision Tree
    • Random Forest
    • K-Nearest Neighbors
  3. Click on the "Prep for Training" button to start training.
  4. For each model:
    • Click on "Calculate Sensitivity" and "Calculate Specificity."
    • Click on "Calculate ROC" and "Calculate AUC."
    • Click on "Calculate Sensitivity at Specificity 0.90."
    • Click on "Calculate Confusion Matrix" button.
